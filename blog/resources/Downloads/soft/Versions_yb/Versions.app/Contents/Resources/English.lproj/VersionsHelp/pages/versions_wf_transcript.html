Transcript

The transcript shows a line by line log of Versions’ interactions with repositories. Every working copy and repository bookmark has its own transcript. You can access the transcript by clicking the “Transcript” tab near the top of the main window, or while Subversion operations are in progress, by clicking the transcript button in the progress dialog that appears.

To clear a transcript type <command + K>, or choose “Clear Transcript Log” from the “View” menu.
