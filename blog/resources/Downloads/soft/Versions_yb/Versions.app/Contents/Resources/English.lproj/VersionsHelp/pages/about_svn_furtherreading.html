Further reading

  • The Subversion project website
    http://subversion.apache.org

    The Subversion project website at Apache. Read release notes, documentation and find links to a wide array of other online resources.

  • Version Control with Subversion
    http://svnbook.red-bean.com

    Probably the web's best resource on Subversion, also available in print from O’Reilly Media. Recommended reading for when you have gone through the Getting started section.

  • A Visual Guide to Version Control
    http://betterexplained.com/...

    A very concise and clear visual explanation of Subversion by Kalid Azad.

  • Subversion for Designers
    http://www.teamtreehouse.com/...

    Excellent article by Beanstalk creator Chris Nagele on the benefits of Subversion for non-programmers. Replace the screenshots in the article with screenshots from Versions in your head to make it even better. ;)
